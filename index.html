About the game

Runemon is a 1-3 player creature-collecting game. The game is currently under development and we highly encourage you to wishlist Runemon on Steam so you won't miss any of our updates.


Story-driven adventure

Put on a backpack and start your journey through the world of Runemon as a newly started monster tamer. Catch new Runemon, battle other adventurers and stop the Shadow Brotherhood's evil plans to rule Runeterra, the world of Runemon.

Creature collecting

As you journey through different environments, you will encounter various types of Runemon. You may also find one of the ancient Runemon that are rumored to live in the unexplored parts of Runeterra. Are you up to catching every Runemon?

Versatile vombat

Build an effective squad by training your Runemon for specific roles, such as attacker, tank, healer or support. Show the power of your Runemon squad in a turn-based 3 vs 3 battle where every buff, debuff, condition and environment matters.

Co-op campaign

Join forces with your friends at any time and face Runeterra's challenges together. Explore the world, complete quests and battle your rivals alongside your friends. You can also challenge your friends to a battle to find out who is the best Runemon tamer in your team.
